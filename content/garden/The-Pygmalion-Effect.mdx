# The Pygmalion Effect

- **A psychological phenomenon**
    * High expectations lead to better performance
    * Low expectations lead to worse
    * Both effects are self-fulfilling prophecies
- **Also called as the Rosenthal effect**
    * Named after the psychologist Robert Rosenthal
- **Named after greek artist [[Pygmalion]]**
